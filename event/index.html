2025

Introduction to Machine Learning and its Applications in Ground Water Resources
The talk introduced Machine Learning (ML) as a tool to solve critical global water challenges. A visual demonstration using the interactive resource “A Visual Introduction to Machine Learning” from R2D3 made key concepts like model training and inference understandable to all attendees.. It covered how ML algorithms like LSTMs and Random Forests are applied to forecast groundwater levels, Saltwater Intrusion (Model & predict saltwater movement), Management Optimization (Find optimal pumping strategy), and Data Gap Filling (Reconstruct missing data). The session highlighted that integrating data-driven ML techniques with hydrology is essential for achieving sustainable water resource management and meeting UN sustainability goals.
Sep 5, 2025 11:00 AM — 12:30 PM School of Civil Engineering, Vellore Institute of Technology
Dr. Manoov R

2024

Hands-on Training in the Evaluation process using Moodle LMS
This talk introduced MOODLE (Modular Object-Oriented Dynamic Learning Environment) and its application in effective course design. It covered strategies for integrating formative and summative assessments using Moodle’s activities and resources. The session demonstrated how to enhance student engagement through gamification (e.g., XP plugin, H5P branching scenarios) and support language teaching using forums, audio assignments, and other plugins. Finally, faculty-specific resources and support steps—particularly within VIT Vellore’s Moodle infrastructure—were highlighted.
Apr 3, 2024 2:00 PM — 5:30 PM Vellore Institute of Technology
Dr. Manoov R

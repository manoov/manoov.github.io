Manoov R, PhD

Assistant Professor Senior of Computer Science & Engg.

Vellore Institute of Technology

Dr. Manoov R is a Senior Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Vellore with over 20 years of academic experience. A dedicated educator and researcher, he specializes in Bioinformatics and Machine Learning. He prioritizes research integrity and ethical practice, principles he models and instills in his teaching and project supervision. At VIT, he plays a key role in managing the university’s Moodle platform for over 30,000+ users and creates resources to help faculty with blended learning. His project supervision has led to patent filings and publications in reputed journals. Beyond academics, Dr. Manoov provides pro bono tech consultancy for NGOs and is certified in research ethics. He encourages students to follow ethics and purpose while developing creative ideas,and develop problem-solving mindset essential for successful entrepreneurship. He is a frequent speaker at faculty workshops, sharing his expertise on educational technology and assessment design. He combines technical skill with a strong commitment to pedagogical innovation and social responsibility. For more information: https://linkedin.com/in/manoov

Introduction to Machine Learning and its Applications in Ground Water Resources
The talk introduced Machine Learning (ML) as a tool to solve critical global water challenges. A visual demonstration using the interactive resource “A Visual Introduction to Machine Learning” from R2D3 made key concepts like model training and inference understandable to all attendees.. It covered how ML algorithms like LSTMs and Random Forests are applied to forecast groundwater levels, Saltwater Intrusion (Model & predict saltwater movement), Management Optimization (Find optimal pumping strategy), and Data Gap Filling (Reconstruct missing data). The session highlighted that integrating data-driven ML techniques with hydrology is essential for achieving sustainable water resource management and meeting UN sustainability goals.

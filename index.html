Manoov R, PhD

Assistant Professor Senior of Computer Science & Engg.

Vellore Institute of Technology

Dr Manoov R is a Senior Assistant Professor at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), specializing in the Department of Internet of Things. His academic and professional background includes extensive experience in computer science and related technologies. His expertise includes machine learning, bioinformatics, computational data mining, semantic web analysis, data integration and knowledge discovery, information extraction, and classification. Dr Manoov is also actively involved in teaching and professional work related to Git, continuous development and integration, Docker, and container-based development. At VIT, he is responsible for Moodle administration, focusing on user authentication and course enrollment. He maintains a webpage titled “Teaching with Moodle” to assist faculty in utilizing Moodle for blended learning. His teaching approach integrates practical applications with theoretical concepts, providing students with hands-on experience in the latest technologies, and he is known for creating academic standards and evaluating new course materials. Beyond academia, Prof. Manoov R has undertaken pro bono consultancy work for charitable organizations and NGOs, showcasing his commitment to social responsibility. Notably, he recently delivered a consultancy project titled ‘Electronic Data Capture and Clinical Research Data Analytics’ for the year 2023-24. Dr. Manoov has shared his expertise through various talks and discussions on formative assessment design to enhance student learning, making contributions to both academia and the wider research community. For more information: https://manoov.github.io/

Consultancy Project: Electronic Data Capture and Clinical Research Data Analytics
In this project, our team successfully configured a REDCap system to support the client’s research endeavors. We began by thoroughly assessing their existing hardware, software, and IT environment (requirements and infrastructure) to guarantee compatibility with REDCap. Following this evaluation, we collaboratively installed and configured the REDCap software on a cloud-based server. Finally, we worked together to customize REDCap to perfectly align with the client’s specific workflows and data management needs.

Introduction to Machine Learning and its Applications in Ground Water Resources
This talk introduced Machine Learning (ML) as a tool to solve critical global water challenges. It covered how ML algorithms like LSTMs and Random Forests are applied to forecast groundwater levels, predict water quality, locate new resources, and optimize management strategies. The session highlighted that integrating data-driven ML techniques with hydrology is essential for achieving sustainable water resource management and meeting UN sustainability goals.

Hands-on Training in the Evaluation process using Moodle LMS
This talk introduced MOODLE (Modular Object-Oriented Dynamic Learning Environment) and its application in effective course design. It covered strategies for integrating formative and summative assessments using Moodle’s activities and resources. The session demonstrated how to enhance student engagement through gamification (e.g., XP plugin, H5P branching scenarios) and support language teaching using forums, audio assignments, and other plugins. Finally, faculty-specific resources and support steps—particularly within VIT Vellore’s Moodle infrastructure—were highlighted.
